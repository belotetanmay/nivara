## 🚀 Welcome to NIVARA v1.0.0

NIVARA is India's premier on-demand mobile recovery platform connecting customers with climate-controlled, sensory-optimized relaxation vans.

### Key Capabilities in v1.0.0:

1. **Customer Mobile & Web Apps**:
   - Location-based discovery & instant booking of 30, 45, or 60-minute recovery sessions.
   - Customizable session preferences (Binaural Beats, Circadian Lighting, Organic Aromatherapy).
   - Real-time van location tracking and active session controls.
   - Google & Native Apple Sign-In alongside email/password and OTP reset.

2. **Wellness Partner (Vendor) Portal**:
   - Fleet vehicle management & real-time availability slot generation.
   - 80% host revenue share tracking and T+3 bank direct deposit payouts.
   - Live booking acceptance/rejection inbox.

3. **Admin Platform Dashboard**:
   - Real-time gross revenue, 80/20 platform split, and operating metrics.
   - Vendor KYC verification & van listing approval workflow.
   - Audit logging & role-based user management.

4. **Razorpay Payments**:
   - Secure checkout supporting UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets with HMAC-SHA256 signature verification.

5. **DPDP Act 2023 Legal Compliance**:
   - Master legal policies, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, Refund Policy, and Grievance Redressal.
